FAQ

Do I need a car?

Not always. Some jobs have rides available. Tell us your situation in the form so we can match you correctly.

Do I need tools?

Most jobs only require basics (gloves, boots). If a job needs tools, we’ll say so in the text.

How do I get jobs?

Make sure your form is complete and your phone number is correct. We text job offers to workers who match the job.

What should I wear?

Work boots, long pants, and gloves are recommended. Safety gear requirements will be listed per job.
